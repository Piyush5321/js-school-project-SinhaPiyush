Human Evolution Timeline

7 Million Years Ago - Sahelanthropus tchadensis

Existed around 7 million years ago. Possibly walked upright with both ape-like and human-like traits.

3.9-2.9 Million Years Ago - Australopithecus afarensis

Lived in East Africa; walked upright; one of the best-known early human species.

2.4-1.4 Million Years Ago - Homo habilis

Among the first tool users; had a larger brain than earlier species.

1.9 Million - 110,000 Years Ago - Homo erectus

Used more advanced tools, stood fully upright, and had body proportions similar to modern humans.

400,000 – 40,000 Years Ago - Homo neanderthalensis

Lived in Europe and western Asia; adapted to cold climates; buried their dead and made tools.

300,000 Years Ago – Present - Homo sapiens

Originated in Africa and spread worldwide; developed complex language, art, and technology.
